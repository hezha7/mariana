About Us

Welcome to Marina Company for Contracting Construction/Ltd, a premier construction and contracting firm established on May 10, 2009. Located in the heart of the Kurdistan Regional Government, within the Republic of Iraq, we have been at the forefront of transforming landscapes and building infrastructures that stand as a testament to our commitment to excellence. Since our inception, we have dedicated ourselves to delivering high-quality construction projects that meet the evolving needs of our community and contribute to the economic growth of the region. Our team of highly skilled professionals, leveraging state-of-the-art technology and innovative practices, ensures that every project we undertake is executed with precision, efficiency, and the utmost attention to detail. At Marina Company, we pride ourselves on our integrity, reliability, and unwavering dedication to our clients. Our projects span across various sectors, including residential, commercial, and industrial constructions, all designed to enhance the quality of life for the people in the Kurdistan region and beyond. As we continue to grow and expand our horizons, our commitment remains steadfast—to build not just structures, but lasting relationships with our clients, stakeholders, and the community at large. Join us as we pave the way towards a brighter, more sustainable future, one project at a time.
